Single Phase Grid-tied Inverter

  • HPK 1~3K The lightest and smallest inverter for 1-3 kW residential PV systems. Perfecting its size in A4 paper and less than 5kg.
  • HPS 1.5~3K HPS Series Single MPPT, 1.5~3kW. Designed for residential PV systems. Compact and elegant with full aluminum die casting.
  • HPS 3~6.5K HPS Series Dual MPPT Version. The benchmark product of single phase grid-tied inverters. Compact in size but robust and reliable.
  • HPS 7-9K HPS 7-9kW with 3 sets of PV inputs. Inheriting all features from residential solutions, bringing extra power to home solar systems with guaranteed safety and quality.

Three Phase Grid-tied Inverter

  • HPT 3~11K HPT is a feature-rich 3-phase inverter series to upgrade your home solar system, covering 3kW to 11kW with the capability of 50% DC overload. HPT is powered with the latest generation of TI DSP, processing rate at 800 millions of instructions, making the inverter more reliable and highly adaptable at complex grid environment.
  • HPT 15~25K The highly adaptive commercial 3-phase inverter from HPT series, covering 15-25kW. Perfect fit for small commercial rooftop systems and a nice application choice for barns and warehouse. HPT 15-25kW shares the same cover with 3-11kW, making the entire series compact and saving up much room in storage and installation.

Energy Storage Inverter

  • HHS 3.68~6K HHS 3.68-6kW is the Hybrid Storage Inverter in Hypontech's Residential Portfolio. Designed as a highly adaptable and flexible option for home PV systems, HHS is integrated with BMS auto-adaption, with Li-ion and Lead Acid options.
  • HBS 3.68~6K HBS is the AC-Coupling Storage Inverter developed by Hypontech, enabling the residential storage option in our portfolio. HBS is designed to be highly adaptable to PV systems, including connections to batteries and grid-tied inverters.
